Teams

Teams are an essential part of the FLuenza Network YAML. I will show you a step by step guide to defining teams with YAML


1) Below the map info section define your teams.

teams:

2) Indent the next section and define your first team.

teams:
  team1:

3) Indent in again and define some of your teams characteristics.

teams:
  team1:
    title: 'Evil'
    color: &4
    spawn: 
      x: 125
      y: 64
      z: 5
      yaw: 0
      pitch: 0

title: 'Evil' defines the title of the team and is the team name that will be shown in the chat.

color: tells the plugin what colour the team messages should be displayed as. Team Messages include but are not limited to: Death Messages, Win/Lose Messages, Scoreboard etc. When defining the colors please use the reference page to get the correct color codes. DO NOT use formatting codes such as for Bold etc.

spawn: tells the plugin where the team will spawn. To spawn in the center of a block you will need to make it a decimal.

4) Now do the same for the other team

teams:
  team1:
    title: 'Evil'
    color: &4
    spawn: 
      x: 125
      y: 64
      z: 5
      yaw: 0
      pitch: 0
  team2:
    title: 'Good'
    color: &a
    spawn: 
      x: 125
      y: 64
      z: 5
      yaw: 0
      pitch: 0
